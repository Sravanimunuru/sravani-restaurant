TATA groups

The Tata Group (/ˈtɑːtɑː/) is an Indian group of companies headquartered in Mumbai.[3][4] Established in 1868, it is India's largest business conglomerate, with products and services in over 150 countries, and operations in 100 countries.[5] There are total 29 publicly listed Tata Group companies with a combined market capitalisation of ₹33.7 trillion (US$403 billion) as of 20 August 2024.[6] Major affiliates include Tata Consultancy Services, Tata Motors, Tata Steel, Tata Power, Titan, Tata Projects, Air India, Indian Hotels Company, Tata Consumer Products, Voltas, Trent, Cromā and BigBasket.[7]
